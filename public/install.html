Install Safarnama on your iPhone in 30 seconds — no App Store needed.

1
Open Safari
Must be Safari — not Chrome or any other browser. Tap the link below.
Open Safarnama in Safari →
2
Tap the Share button
The Share button is the square with an arrow pointing up — at the bottom of your screen.
Look for this icon at the bottom of Safari
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share sheet until you see "Add to Home Screen". Tap it.
"Add to Home Screen" — with a + icon
4
Tap Add
The name will show as "Safarnama". Tap Add in the top right corner. Done!
The compass icon will appear on your home screen. Tap it to open Safarnama — it works just like an app, with no browser bar.
Free · No Play Store needed

Install Safarnama on your Android phone in under a minute.

1
Open Chrome
Open Google Chrome on your Android phone and go to the link below.
Open Safarnama in Chrome →
2
Tap the three dots menu
In the top right corner of Chrome, tap the three vertical dots ⋮
Three dots at the top right corner
3
Tap "Add to Home screen"
You'll see this option in the dropdown menu. Tap it.
"Add to Home screen" option
4
Tap Add
Confirm by tapping Add. The Safarnama icon appears on your home screen.
The compass icon will appear on your Android home screen. Tap it anytime to open Safarnama — it works offline too once loaded.
Prefer an APK file?

If someone sent you an APK file directly, enable "Install unknown apps" in your phone Settings → Security, then open the APK file to install.
